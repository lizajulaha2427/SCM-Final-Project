Other Finance

BUDGETING

Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to spend your money. This spending plan is called a budget.

MORTGAGE

A mortgage is a type of loan used to purchase or maintain a home, land, or other types of real estate.

INVESTMENT

Investment definition is an asset acquired or invested in to build wealth and save money from the hard earned income or appreciation.

ESTATE PLANNING

Estate planning involves determining how an individual's assets will be preserved, managed, and distributed after death.

DEBT REPAYMENT

A debt is the sum of money that is borrowed for a certain period of time and is to be return along with the interest.

ACCOUNTING

Accounting is the process of keeping track of all financial transactions within a business, such as any money coming in and money going out.
